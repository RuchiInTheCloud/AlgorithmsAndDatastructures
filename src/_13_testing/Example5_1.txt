Test a pen

Who will use? Children
What will they do with it? Drawing
On what? Clothing
What kind of tip? Intended to wash off

Different components of the object that need to be tested:
Fact check
Intended use: Verify it is felt tip and ink is one of allowed colors.
- Drawing: Does pen write on clothing
- Washing: Does it wash off of clothing (even after being present for a long period of time)? Wash off in cold, warm and
cold water?
Safety: Is the pen safe for children=
Unintended uses: Children may write on other surface. They may stomp on the pen. Throw it. How does the pen behave does
it hold up.
